About Us

Bonvent cider is a new cider maker, cider room and event space in Hancock, Maine. We pick the wild stuff, the ugly stuff, the hidden stuff and and turn it into a mouthful of sunshine that harkens those cozy feels of 60 degree days, crisp, colorful fall leaves and warm hoodies. Opening in 2024, owners Anne and Mathias are excited to welcome you to celebrate the wilder part of Maine, in a glass.
